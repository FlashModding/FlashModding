Head of candycorn wants some spooky sweets!
Try out this halloween themed head of candy mod, it contains new candy types, and spooky atmosphere
Candycorn - very tall and bright traingle candy, gives low score
Chocolate ball - round and dark, tasty chocolate
Pumpkin - wide rectangular and very heavy, symbol of halloween
Gummy mansion - purple mansion with very little (absoluteley zero) details
Gummy worm - tiny gummy worms, give ton of score if you manage to collect them
Spooky soda - green bottles of very fizzy soda, it bounces a loot and gives much score!
Hay - just small boxes of hay, who even put this into candy basket?
Blood orbs - special treat for vampires, or those who love meat meals
