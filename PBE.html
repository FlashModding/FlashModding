Expansion is the final Perfect Balance mod by Zack Max, and quite possibly the most major. It is exactly what it says in the name, expanding the game to be quite large
There are many things you can play other than main game such as Mini-Games, Hybrid Mode, and Endless.
There are many new custom shapes, custom items, custom block variants such as Bouncy blocks.
